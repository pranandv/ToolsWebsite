About Snake Game

Snake is one of the most iconic video games ever created, first appearing in arcades in the late 1970s and becoming widely popular on Nokia mobile phones in the 1990s.

How to play:

  1. Use the arrow keys on your keyboard (or the on-screen controls on mobile) to move the snake
  2. Eat the red food to grow longer and earn points
  3. Avoid hitting the walls or running into your own tail
  4. Try to achieve the highest score possible

Controls:

  • Desktop: Arrow keys (←, →, ↑, ↓)
  • Mobile: On-screen arrow buttons
  • Start/Pause buttons to control the game

Snake is not just fun—it's also a great way to improve your reflexes, spatial awareness, and strategic thinking. How long can you make your snake grow?
