About Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a classic paper-and-pencil game for two players who take turns marking X or O in a 3Ã—3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner.

How to play:

  1. Select a game mode: Easy AI, Hard AI, or Two Players
  2. X always goes first (this is you in AI mode)
  3. Players take turns placing their symbols (X or O) on empty cells
  4. The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins
  5. If all cells are filled and no one has won, the game is a tie

Game Modes:

  • Easy AI: Computer makes random moves
  • Hard AI: Computer uses strategy to make optimal moves
  • Two Players: Play against a friend on the same device

Strategy tips:

  • Try to take the center square early if possible
  • Block your opponent when they have two in a row
  • Create "forks" where you have two potential winning moves
  • Watch for diagonal opportunities that are easy to miss

Despite its simple rules, Tic Tac Toe offers valuable lessons in strategic thinking and planning ahead, making it a timeless game enjoyed by people of all ages.
