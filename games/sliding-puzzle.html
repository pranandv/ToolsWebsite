About Sliding Puzzle

The Sliding Puzzle is a classic problem-solving game that has been entertaining and challenging people for generations.

How to play:

  1. The puzzle consists of a 4×4 grid with 15 numbered tiles and one empty space
  2. Click any tile adjacent to the empty space to move it into that space
  3. Rearrange the tiles to place them in numerical order (1-15) with the empty space in the bottom right corner
  4. Try to solve the puzzle in as few moves as possible

Benefits of playing:

  • Improves problem-solving skills
  • Enhances spatial awareness
  • Develops strategic planning abilities
  • Exercises working memory

The sliding puzzle was invented in the 1870s and has remained a popular brain teaser ever since. The 15-puzzle (4×4) version you're playing has over 10 trillion possible arrangements, but only half of them are actually solvable!
