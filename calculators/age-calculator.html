About Age Calculator

The Age Calculator is a simple tool that computes your exact age in years, months, and days based on your date of birth.

How it works:

  1. Enter your date of birth using the calendar picker
  2. Optionally, select a specific calculation date (defaults to today)
  3. Click "Calculate Age" to see the results

What you'll learn:

  • Your precise age in years, months, and days
  • Fun facts about your age
  • How many days you've been alive
  • Upcoming milestone birthdays

Common uses:

  • Calculate exact age for official documents
  • Determine eligibility for age-restricted activities
  • Plan milestone birthday celebrations
  • Calculate age at a future date (like retirement)
  • Determine exact age difference between two people

The Age Calculator takes into account leap years and varying month lengths to provide an accurate calculation of your age.
