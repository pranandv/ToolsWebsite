About Pedigree Age Calculator

The Pedigree Age Calculator converts your dog's age to human years using a more accurate method than the old "multiply by 7" rule.

How it works:

  1. Enter your dog's age in years (decimals allowed for puppies)
  2. Select your dog's size category
  3. Click "Calculate Human Age" to see the results

The science behind the calculation:

This calculator uses a more accurate aging method that accounts for:

  • Dogs age faster during their first two years of life
  • pedigree age calculator
  • Small dogs generally live longer than large dogs
  • Giant breeds tend to age more rapidly after adulthood

Dog size categories:

  • Small: Up to 20 pounds (Chihuahua, Yorkshire Terrier, Pomeranian)
  • Medium: 21-50 pounds (Beagle, Cocker Spaniel, Border Collie)
  • Large: 51-100 pounds (Labrador, German Shepherd, Boxer)
  • Giant: Over 100 pounds (Great Dane, Saint Bernard, Newfoundland)

Understanding your dog's age in human terms can help you better anticipate their changing needs as they grow and age.
