About BMI Calculator

The Body Mass Index (BMI) calculator is a tool that assesses your body weight relative to your height, providing an indication of whether you're at a healthy weight.

How it works:

  1. Select your preferred unit system (metric or imperial)
  2. Enter your weight and height measurements
  3. Provide your age and gender for more accurate recommendations
  4. Click "Calculate BMI" to see your results

Understanding BMI:

  • BMI is a screening tool, not a diagnostic of body fatness or health
  • The same BMI calculation is used for both men and women
  • For adults 20 years and older, BMI categories are not age-specific
  • Athletes may have a high BMI due to muscle mass, not excess fat

BMI Formula:

For metric units: BMI = weight(kg) / height(m)²

For imperial units: BMI = 703 × weight(lb) / height(in)²

Note: BMI is just one indicator of health and should be used alongside other measurements. Always consult with healthcare professionals for proper evaluation of your weight status and health risks.
