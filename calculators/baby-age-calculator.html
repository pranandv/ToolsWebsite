About Baby Age Calculator

The Baby Age Calculator helps parents track their baby's age in different units including months, weeks, and days.

How it works:

  1. Enter your baby's date of birth
  2. Optionally, select a calculation date (defaults to today)
  3. Click "Calculate Baby's Age" to see the results
  4. View your baby's age in multiple formats

Why use this calculator:

  • Track your baby's development milestones accurately
  • Convert between weeks and months for medical appointments
  • Share your baby's exact age with family and friends
  • Record growth progress for baby books and journals

The first two years of a baby's life are full of rapid changes, and many parents prefer to track age in weeks or months rather than years. This calculator makes it easy to know exactly how old your little one is in whatever unit you need.
